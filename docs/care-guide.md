# Glassware Care Guide

## Daily Care

1. **Wash immediately**: Rinse glasses right after use
2. **Hand wash preferred**: Use mild detergent and warm water
3. **No dishwasher**: Harsh detergents damage crystal and coatings
4. **Dry immediately**: Use lint-free cloth to prevent water spots

## Deep Cleaning

- **Mineral deposits**: Soak in 50/50 vinegar and water for 15 minutes
- **Lipstick marks**: Rub with baking soda paste, rinse well
- **Cloudy glass**: Soak in vinegar solution, then polish

## Storage

- **Stemmed glasses**: Store upright, never rim-down
- **Tumblers**: Can store upside down to keep dust out
- **Crystal**: Store in padded racks or individual boxes
- **Avoid stacking**: Prevents chipping and scratching

## Polishing

1. Hold glass by bowl (not stem) over steam
2. Use lint-free microfiber cloth
3. Polish with slight rotation
4. Check for spots in good light

## Temperature Tips

- **Chill glasses**: Keep cocktail glasses in freezer 30+ minutes
- **Warm glasses**: For hot drinks, pre-warm with hot water
- **Avoid thermal shock**: Never move glass between extreme temperatures
- **Crystal warning**: Never pour hot liquid into cold crystal

## When to Replace

- Chipped rims (dangerous and affects drinking experience)
- Deep scratches that harbor bacteria
- Cloudiness that won't polish out
- Cracks (even hairline cracks can fail)
